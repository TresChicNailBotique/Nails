TreatmentsGel Nails and Pedicure Services

Nail Preparation

Soak off (if necessary), push Back Cuticles, gentle buff to remove shine, shape and apply nail prep protein and rubber base to prepare nails for required service.

Gel Overlay

Applied like Polish, dried under an LED/UV Light & longer lasting than Nail Polish. (Non Soak Off, removal done with E-File completely safe and non damaging to natural nail)

French Overlay

Applied with Grace’ Clear Building Gel and White Building Gel to create a perfect sculptured shape, these building gels are self leveling which provides for a thinner and more natural finish. Dried under LED/UV Light. (Three colours to choice from – Nude, Pink, Misty or clear) – NON SOAK OFF

Gel Sculpture (Extension)

Applied with Grace’ building gel and nail form to create and sculpt the perfect length extension and shape. Self levelling gel which provides for a thinner and more natural finish. Cured in LED/UV Light. Gel Polish colour applied and Cured in LED/UV Light. NON SOAK OF

Tips (Full)

Applied to Natural Nail with Rubber base, It serves as the “GLUE” to create perfect length extension, the Rubber base is also designed to give extra strength to brittle, thin or splitting nails and provides Longer longevity up to 5 weeks wear. Gel Polish colour applied and Cured in LED/UV Light. NON SOAK OFF

Tips (Half)

Applied to Free Edge of Natural Nail with Rubber Base, to create perfect length extension and sculptured with Building Gel strengthen nail. Gel Colour applied and cured in LED/UV Light. NON SOAK OFF
